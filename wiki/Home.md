# MonkeyWifHat Wiki

Welcome to the official field manual for MonkeyWifHat.

The premise is simple: monkey, hat, unreasonable confidence. The execution should be equally simple: clear docs, visible disclosures, repeatable branding, and no fake mythology about guaranteed outcomes.

This wiki is written as the source of truth for the project voice, structure, and launch discipline.
