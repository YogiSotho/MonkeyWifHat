# Glossary

## Allocation

The percentage of total token supply assigned to a given purpose or wallet category.

## Community Allocation

Tokens reserved for public distribution, incentives, community activity, or ecosystem participation.

## Fixed Supply

A token supply that does not increase after launch.

## Freeze Authority

A token permission that may allow transfers or accounts to be frozen, depending on implementation. If it exists, the project should explain why.

## Liquidity

The token inventory paired for trading so market participants can buy and sell.

## Mint Authority

A permission that allows additional tokens to be created. If this authority exists, the public should know who controls it and when it will be revoked.

## Multisig

A wallet or control mechanism that requires multiple approvals rather than a single person acting alone.

## Meme Token

A token whose primary value proposition is culture, community, identity, and internet-native branding rather than deep technical utility.

## Vesting

A schedule that controls when allocated tokens become available to a team, contributor, or stakeholder.

## Wallet Disclosure

A public explanation of what specific project-controlled wallets are for and how they are intended to be used.
