# Roadmap

## Overview

The roadmap should stay narrow, credible, and easy to verify.

Do not inflate the roadmap just to create drama.

## Phase 1: Establish The Monkey

- finalize name, ticker, and visual identity
- publish wiki and launch documentation
- prepare official channels
- prepare first content pack

## Phase 2: Launch Cleanly

- publish token details and disclosures
- open community channels with pinned FAQ
- launch with consistent official links
- maintain moderation and correction process

## Phase 3: Feed The Meme

- run recurring meme prompts
- rotate hat themes
- spotlight community creators
- expand visual asset library

## Phase 4: Keep Only What Works

- retain recurring content formats that travel
- remove bloated messaging
- simplify public materials when confusion appears
- publish updates only when there is something real to say

## Roadmap Rule

If a roadmap item sounds impressive but cannot be explained in one sentence, it probably does not belong here.
