# Brand and Lore

## Core Brand Idea

MonkeyWifHat only works if the joke lands in under two seconds.

The whole stack is:

- monkey
- hat
- unreasonable self-belief
- zero over-explaining

If any asset needs a paragraph to justify itself, the asset is wrong.

## Naming

Primary name: `MonkeyWifHat`

Possible ticker candidates:

- `MWH`
- `MONKE`
- `MWHAT`

The final ticker should be selected for clarity, conflict avoidance, and how well it survives being repeated by people with poor attention spans.

## Tone Guide

The voice should feel:

- chaotic on purpose
- confident without lying
- meme-native, not corporate
- short enough to quote-post
- repetitive enough to become recognizable

## Visual Language

The world of the project should revolve around a few reusable building blocks:

- rotating hat variants
- mugshot-style monkey portraits
- rough collage edits
- reaction templates
- a simple icon that reads well at profile-image size

## Sample Taglines

- Monkey sees hat.
- Hat stays on.
- Serious chain. Unserious monkey.
- Built on bad ideas and good documentation.
- No prophecy. Just monkey with hat.

## Lore Rule

The lore should stay light.

Do not force a giant fictional universe around a joke that already works. The best version is a thin layer of myth on top of a repeatable image.

Base story:

> MonkeyWifHat began when somebody put a hat on a monkey and everyone else acted like this explained the market. From that point on, the hat was no longer an accessory. It was policy.
