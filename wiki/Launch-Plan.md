# Launch Plan

## Objective

The launch should feel funny from the outside and disciplined from the inside.

That means the brand can be chaotic, but the process cannot be.

## Pre-Launch Requirements

Before any public launch date is announced, have the following ready:

- brand assets finalized
- official accounts established
- tokenomics page drafted
- wallet disclosures prepared
- token details verified
- FAQ written
- moderation coverage ready

## Launch Sequence

1. Publish the wiki and landing page.
2. Publish tokenomics and wallet disclosures.
3. Publish the official links list.
4. Publish the token address only after final verification.
5. Open community channels with pinned FAQ and risk statement.
6. Start content cadence that matches the published story.

## Messaging Rule

Say what the project is. Do not say what excitable people want it to be.

Recommended themes:

- transparent meme launch
- simple structure
- community remixability
- entertainment and participation

## Operational Checklist

- verify official accounts
- verify token identifier and address
- verify allocation statement matches actual setup
- verify moderators have FAQ and escalation guidance
- verify disclaimer language is visible everywhere that matters

## Red Flags

Pause the process if any of these are true:

- allocations are undocumented
- wallet roles are unclear
- official links disagree with each other
- token details are being spread informally before confirmation
- marketing copy overstates reality
